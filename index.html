Meet the Designer

The heart behind the florals

CassCustomFlorals is a floral design studio that blends classic elegance with modern artistry. We curate pieces for the vision and personality of your dream event, combining texture, structure, and visual storytelling through flowers. We don't just make pretty arrangements — we create contemporary floral experiences that are intentional, sculptural, and alive.

After years of flower arranging as a hobby, Cassidy officially launched CassCustomFlorals because of the simple joy her designs bring to an event. With a background in engineering and consulting, she understands the attention to detail and passion it takes to transform an idea into reality.

Cassidy's favorite element about her brand is how much relief hiring a trusted florist brings to a client. When you choose Cassidy as 'The One', she takes personal responsibility to curate your floral vision with you, and for you.

✦ Working out of her home studio in Lynn, MA
